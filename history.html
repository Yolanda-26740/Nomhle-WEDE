How We Started

CaseCraze was founded in 2024 with a simple vision: to create a unique and stylish phone case brand that blends fashion, affordability, and protection. The idea began when the founder noticed a gap in the South African market customers either had to choose between expensive designer cases or low-quality generic covers. CaseCraze was built to bridge that gap. What started as a small side project with just a handful of designs quickly grew into a business concept that resonated with young, tech-savvy individuals. By focusing on customer-driven designs, trendy styles, and reliable protection, CaseCraze set itself apart as more than just a phone accessory shop it became a lifestyle brand. Within the first year, CaseCraze established an online presence through social media marketing and word-of-mouth referrals, attracting attention for its fun branding and affordable prices.
